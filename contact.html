Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website? ▼

A standard website typically takes 5–10 business days. Landing pages can be ready in 2–3 days. Complex e-commerce projects may take 2–4 weeks.

Do you offer payment plans? ▼

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans. Typically, 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. Contact us to discuss options that work for your budget.

Will my website work on mobile phones? ▼

Absolutely. All our websites are mobile-first and fully responsive — they look and work great on any device or screen size.

Do you provide support after project delivery? ▼

Yes, we provide 30 days of free support after delivery. After that, we offer affordable monthly maintenance packages.

Can I update my website myself? ▼

We can build your site with an easy-to-use content management system (CMS) and train you to update content, images, and text without any coding knowledge.
